About us

Our beautiful little coffee café was born from the deep connection Ethiopian people share with coffee. We wanted to bring that tradition into a modern space offering the full Ethiopian coffee ceremony experience, while also making it accessible through delivery.

We’re more than a café we’re a small circle of people who love coffee, culture, and connection.

We’re glad you’re a part of it.
